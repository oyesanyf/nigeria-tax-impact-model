DIRECT DATA DOWNLOAD LINKS (NBS & CBN)
========================================

1. GDP Growth Data (National Bureau of Statistics)
   -------------------------------------------------
   * Website: https://nigerianstat.gov.ng/elibrary
   * Direct Search: "Gross Domestic Product"
   * Instructions: 
     - Go to the link above.
     - Look for the report titled "Nigerian Gross Domestic Product Report Q[x] 20[xx]".
     - Download the XLSX (Excel) file attached to the report.
     - Look for the "Real GDP Growth" sheet.

2. Crude Oil Prices (Central Bank of Nigeria)
   -------------------------------------------------
   * Website: https://www.cbn.gov.ng/rates/crudeoil.asp
   * Note: This page shows recent daily prices.
   * For Historical Data:
     - Go to https://www.cbn.gov.ng/documents/statbulletin.asp
     - Download the latest "Statistical Bulletin" (Excel format).
     - Look for the "Public Finance" or "Petroleum Sector" section for historical Bonny Light prices.

3. Tax Revenue Data (NBS - CIT & VAT)
   -------------------------------------------------
   * Website: https://nigerianstat.gov.ng/elibrary
   * Company Income Tax (CIT):
     - Search for "Company Income Tax".
     - Download "Company Income Tax by Sector Q[x] 20[xx]".
   * Value Added Tax (VAT):
     - Search for "Sectoral Distribution of Value Added Tax".
     - Download the report for the relevant quarter.

4. Inflation (Optional)
   -------------------------------------------------
   * Website: https://nigerianstat.gov.ng/elibrary
   * Search for: "Consumer Price Index (CPI)"
